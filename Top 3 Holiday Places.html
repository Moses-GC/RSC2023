Top 3 Holiday Places
Holidays are the best days to be with family and friends. Everyone eagerly observes the calendar for holidays. Picking good places to visit can be quite challenging. But don't worry, we are here to help you get the best possible places. These 3 places will guarantee you to have fun whether you are traveling with family, friends, or even by yourself.

Science Centre Singapore are the route to one such interactive exhibit. Come along with your friends and family to this magnificent Science Centre and learn some amazing theories of science together. Enjoy your time exploring a nature park that sprawls across 101 hectares of beautiful land in the Central Region of Singapore. Gardens By The Bay lies beautifully with the surrounding of the Marina Reservoir. Get to explore the world's tallest indoor waterfall and see a serene cloud forest on your trip here. A perfect place to visit with your family and loved ones while being in Singapore. One of the best man-made structures to house the beauty of flora and fauna. You will find waterfront gardens and conservatories which makes the place one of a kind destination to visit. Explore many more things at Gardens By the Bay in Singapore.

Address: 18 Marina Gardens Dr, Singapore 018953

Opening hours: 5am-2am daily

Universal studious is well-known and the name itself reminds us of the wonderful and amazing movies produced by this production house. Imagine a theme park that brings all of its characters and stories to life. The theme park houses different areas like Hollywood, New York, Sci-fi City, Ancient Egypt, The Lost World, Far Far Away, and Madagascar, each having a certain significance in the real world, as well as in the movies. There are amazing and wonderful rides such as Transformers: The Ride, Accelerator, Revenge of the Mummy: The Ride, Canopy Flyer, Dino Soarin, Enchanted Airways, Magic Potion Spin, Madagascar: A Crate Adventure, and many more.
